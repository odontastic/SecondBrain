2.  **Turn Away:** Ignore or miss the bid. You don\'t respond.

3.  **Turn Against:** Reject the bid with irritation or anger.

Couples who thrive \"turn towards\" each other\'s bids over 80% of the
time. Couples who divorce do it less than 30% of the time.

Your wife\'s yelling is a very loud, desperate, and painful bid for
