## Template Usage Guide

### When to Use This Template
- **New projects** with defined goals and deadlines
- **Active work items** requiring structured planning
- **Complex tasks** that need milestone tracking
- **Collaborative efforts** involving multiple people or resources

### Customization Guidelines
1. **Adapt phases** to match your specific project workflow
2. **Adjust milestone structure** based on project complexity
3. **Modify risk categories** to fit your context
4. **Scale complexity** to match project scope

### Best Practices
- **Update regularly** - Keep status, metrics, and progress current
- **Link actively** - Connect to relevant areas and resources
- **Review milestones** - Check progress against planned timelines
- **Document learnings** - Capture insights for future projects
