- Status: `status/IN-PROGRESS`, `status/EDITING`, `status/PUBLISHED`
- Temporal: `@today`, `@tomorrow`, `@this-week`, `@next-week`
- Theme: `theme/mindfulness`, `theme/stoicism`, `theme/CBT`
- TK: `TK/` (to come - needs work)
