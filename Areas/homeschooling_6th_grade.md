# Area: Homeschooling - 6th Grade

**Status:** Active  
**Standard to Maintain:** Comprehensive, engaging education for 12-year-old son

---

## Ongoing Responsibilities

### Daily Homeschool Management
- Curriculum oversight and instruction
- Daily check-ins (15 minutes undivided attention)
- Lesson planning and preparation
- Progress tracking
- Assignment management

### Educational Standards
- Meet or exceed grade-level standards
- Engaging, age-appropriate content
- Support son's interests (not just curriculum)
- Foster love of learning

### Presence & Connection
- 15 minutes daily undivided attention (from action plan)
- Ask about HIS interests, not just curriculum
- No multitasking during homeschool time
- Model presence and engagement

- Ask about HIS interests (not just curriculum)
- Listen to his thoughts about what he's learning

- Show interest in his perspective

**Remember:** Presence before curriculum. Connection before completion.
