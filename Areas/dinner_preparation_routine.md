- [ ] **Herbs & Aromatics:**
  - [ ] Chop garlic, onions

- [ ] **Equipment Prep:**

  - [ ] Set out utensils needed
  - [ ] Prepare serving dishes if applicable

## Troubleshooting

### Missing Ingredients
- **Option 1:** Substitute with what's available
- **Option 2:** Adjust meal plan for that day
- **Option 3:** Quick trip to store (if urgent and nearby)
- **Prevention:** Better weekly planning and shopping

### Running Behind Schedule
- **Simplify:** Choose quicker meal option
- **Adjust timing:** Start routine earlier if needed
- **Communicate:** Let family know if dinner will be late

### Family Schedule Changes
- **Early dinner?** Start routine earlier
- **Late dinner?** Adjust timing or keep warm
- **Fewer people?** Adjust portions

---

## Tips for Efficiency

1. **Weekly Planning:** Plan all meals on Sunday
2. **Batch Prep:** Prep ingredients for multiple days when possible
3. **Leftovers Strategy:** Plan leftovers for lunches or next-day dinners
4. **Clean As You Go:** Prevents overwhelming cleanup after
5. **Mise-en-Place:** Invest time in prep = smoother cooking
6. **Time Management:** Know how long each step takes
7. **Recipe Familiarity:** Know recipes well or have them easily accessible
