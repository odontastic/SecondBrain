# Area: Personal Style & Presentation

**Status:** Active  
**Standard to Maintain:** Stylish, cohesive, well-groomed personal presentation that reflects confidence and intentionality

---

## Ongoing Responsibilities

### Daily Style & Wardrobe
- Choose outfits that are coordinated and stylish
- Ensure clothing matches and creates cohesive looks
- Select appropriate attire for occasion and context
- Maintain wardrobe organization for easy access

### Grooming & Maintenance
- Daily hair grooming and styling
- Hair color maintenance (when applicable)
- Facial hair grooming (if applicable)
- Nail care and maintenance
- Overall hygiene and presentation

### Accessorizing
- Select appropriate accessories (watch, jewelry, etc.)
- Coordinate accessories with outfit
- Ensure accessories enhance rather than distract

### Wardrobe Management
- Organize wardrobe for easy selection
- Maintain clothing in good condition
- Track seasonal wardrobe needs
- Identify gaps in wardrobe (items needed)
- Plan outfit combinations in advance when helpful

---

## Style Principles

### Cohesion & Coordination
- Colors should complement each other
- Fit should be appropriate (not too tight, not too loose)
- Style should be consistent with personal aesthetic
- Consider occasion and context when selecting

### Quality & Maintenance
- Keep clothing clean, pressed, and in good repair
- Invest in quality pieces that last
- Maintain shoes and accessories
- Regular wardrobe review and culling

### Personal Expression
- Style should reflect authentic self
- Comfort should not be sacrificed for style
- Confidence comes from feeling good in what you wear
- Allow for personal preferences and tastes

## Style Decision Framework

### When Selecting Outfit
1. **Occasion/Context:** What is the setting?
2. **Weather:** What are conditions like?
3. **Comfort:** Will this feel good all day?
4. **Coordination:** Do pieces work together?
5. **Authenticity:** Does this reflect me?

### When Adding to Wardrobe
1. **Versatility:** Can it be worn multiple ways?
2. **Quality:** Will it last and maintain appearance?
3. **Fit:** Does it fit well and look good?
4. **Coordination:** Does it work with existing wardrobe?
5. **Value:** Is it worth the investment?
