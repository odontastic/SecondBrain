# Area: Pool Maintenance

**Status:** Active  
**Standard to Maintain:** Clean, safe, properly balanced pool

---

## Ongoing Responsibilities

### Daily/Almost Daily
- Pool cleaning (skim, vacuum, brush as needed)
- Visual inspection
- Maintain cleanliness

### Weekly (Weekends - Discounted Electricity)
- Check chemical levels (pH, chlorine, alkalinity, etc.)
- Balance chemicals as needed
- Test water quality
- Run filtration and circulation pump during discounted hours

### Periodic
- Filter cleaning and maintenance
- Equipment inspection
- Seasonal care

---

## Standards
- Pool clean and clear daily
- Chemical levels balanced weekly
- Safe for swimming
- Cost-effective (use discounted electricity rates when possible)

---

## Schedule
- **Daily cleaning:** As needed (almost daily)
- **Chemical testing/balancing:** Weekend (discounted electricity rates)
- **Pump operation:** Coordinate with discounted electricity windows

---

## Notes
- Weekend chemical maintenance saves on electricity costs
- Daily cleaning prevents major problems
- Safety is priority (proper chemical balance)
