# Maintenance Schedule for Projects and Areas

## Overview
This document outlines the maintenance schedule for regular review of Projects and Areas in the LifeOS system.

## Review Frequency
- **Projects**: Review active projects weekly, assess project priorities monthly, and archive completed projects quarterly.
- **Areas**: Conduct a quick status check of high-priority areas weekly, comprehensive area review monthly, deep dive into each area quarterly, and major area evaluation annually.

## Review Process
1. Review the status and progress of Projects and Areas according to the scheduled frequency.
2. Update the relevant Markdown files with any changes or new information.
3. Assess the effectiveness of current Projects and Areas and make adjustments as necessary.

## Maintenance Tasks
- Regularly review and update the maintenance schedule as needed.
- Ensure that all Projects and Areas are properly categorized and tagged.
- Continuously improve the review process based on feedback and outcomes.

## Responsibilities
- The user is responsible for conducting regular reviews and maintaining the Projects and Areas.
- The system will provide reminders and notifications for upcoming reviews.

## Tracking Progress
- Use the `last_modified` field in the YAML frontmatter to track when Projects and Areas were last updated.
- Maintain a log of review activities and outcomes for future reference.
