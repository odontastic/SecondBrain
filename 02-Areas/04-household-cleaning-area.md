# Household Cleaning & Organization

## Area Overview
As part of household management responsibilities, I maintain a clean, organized, and welcoming home environment through regular cleaning routines and organizational systems.

## Current Responsibilities

### Daily Cleaning Tasks
- **Kitchen maintenance**: Post-meal cleaning, countertop sanitization, sink care
- **Bathroom upkeep**: Daily surface cleaning, restocking supplies
- **Living areas**: Straightening, dusting, floor maintenance
- **High-traffic areas**: Regular attention to entryways, hallways, main living spaces

### Weekly Deep Cleaning
- **Thorough kitchen cleaning**: Appliance cleaning, cabinet organization, floor deep cleaning
- **Bathroom deep cleaning**: Shower/tub deep cleaning, toilet sanitization, mirror cleaning
- **Bedroom organization**: Bedding changes, closet organization, dust removal
- **Living space maintenance**: Furniture cleaning, window care, comprehensive floor cleaning

### Monthly Organization Tasks
- **Pantry organization**: Food rotation, expiration date checks, organization systems
- **Closet organization**: Seasonal clothing rotation, donation preparation
- **Storage areas**: Garage, closets, and utility areas organization
- **Appliance maintenance**: Deep cleaning of all household appliances

## Cleaning Supplies & Equipment
- **Essential cleaning products**: All-purpose cleaners, disinfectants, glass cleaner
- **Equipment inventory**: Vacuum cleaner, mop, buckets, microfiber cloths
- **Specialized tools**: Grout brushes, appliance cleaning tools, organizers
- **Safety equipment**: Gloves, masks when using harsh chemicals

## Organization Systems
- **Daily maintenance routine**: Quick pickup and cleaning throughout the day
- **Weekly deep cleaning schedule**: Systematic approach to all areas
- **Monthly organization projects**: Decluttering and reorganizing systems
- **Seasonal cleaning**: Major maintenance and reorganization cycles
