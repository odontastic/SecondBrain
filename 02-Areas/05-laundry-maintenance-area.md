# Laundry Maintenance & Clothing Care

## Area Overview
As part of household management responsibilities, I handle all laundry operations, clothing care, and textile maintenance to ensure proper clothing inventory and care.

## Current Responsibilities

### Regular Laundry Operations
- **Daily laundry sorting**: Separating whites, colors, delicates, and special care items
- **Washing and drying**: Operating washing machine and dryer with proper settings
- **Folding and organizing**: Proper folding techniques and storage organization
- **Stain treatment**: Pre-treatment of stains and special care for delicate items

### Clothing Care & Maintenance
- **Clothing inventory**: Tracking of clothing items, sizes, and condition
- **Seasonal clothing rotation**: Switching out seasonal garments and accessories
- **Special care items**: Handling of delicate fabrics, formal wear, and special textiles
- **Repair and maintenance**: Basic clothing repairs and professional care when needed

### Linen & Towel Management
- **Bed linens**: Regular washing, rotation, and replacement scheduling
- **Bath towels**: Washing, rotation, and soft towel maintenance
- **Kitchen linens**: Dish towels, tablecloths, and other kitchen textiles
- **Special occasion linens**: Care and storage of formal linens and special items

## Laundry Equipment & Supplies
- **Washing machine and dryer**: Regular maintenance and proper operation
- **Laundry detergents and supplies**: Stock of appropriate cleaning products

## Clothing Care Guidelines
- **Sorting procedures**: Proper separation by color, fabric, and care requirements
- **Washing instructions**: Temperature settings, cycle selections, and special care

- **Storage organization**: Proper hanging, folding, and storage techniques
