## Area Management Framework
- **Standards**: Quality levels to maintain in each area
- **KPIs**: Measurable indicators of success
- **Review Frequency**: How often to assess area health
- **Maintenance Tasks**: Regular actions required
- **Improvement Areas**: Focus points for development

### Review Schedule
- **Weekly**: Quick status check of high-priority areas
- **Monthly**: Comprehensive area review
- **Quarterly**: Deep dive into each area
- **Annually**: Major area evaluation and goal setting

### Area Status Indicators
- **Active**: Well-managed, meeting standards
- **Needs Attention**: Requiring focus or improvement
- **Well Maintained**: Exceeding expectations
- **Under Review**: Being evaluated or restructured
