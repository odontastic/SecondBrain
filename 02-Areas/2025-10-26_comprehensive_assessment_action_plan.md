## WHAT YOU NEED TO WORK ON (Priority Order)

### TIER 1: IMMEDIATE & FOUNDATIONAL (Next 30 Days)

#### 1. **DEVELOP YOUR VOICE** 
**The problem**: You go silent when criticized, believing you "don't have the right" to speak

**What to do**:
- **Use the First-Aid Kit phrases** from Couples Communication Toolkit
  - Start with: "You're right. The pressure on you is immense."
  - Level up to: "I can see how scary that is."
  - Ultimate: "I want to be on your team with this."
- **Practice the Sigh Playbook**: When she sighs (pressure valve), acknowledge it
  - "That sounded like a heavy sigh."
  - "You okay?"
- **Speak even when uncomfortable**: Your silence is hurting her more than imperfect words would

**Success measure**: Can you stay in the room during conflict and say ONE validating phrase before withdrawing?

---

#### 2. **HAVE THE CONVERSATION** 
**The problem**: You're carrying three sources of resentment that you've never voiced

**What to do**: Choose ONE conversation this week

**OPTION 1 - The Resentment Conversation** (Recommended):
*"I need to tell you something that's hard for me to say. I feel invisible. I cook, clean, take care of the kids, manage the household - and I don't think you see it. I know you carry an enormous financial burden and I'm grateful. But I need you to know: I'm working too. And when you criticize me, I shut down because I feel like nothing I do matters to you."*

**OPTION 2 - The Vulnerability Conversation**:
*"I need help. I know I withdraw when you get upset with me. I know that makes you feel alone. The truth is - I feel hopeless and ashamed in those moments. I don't know how to handle conflict with you. I need us to learn together how to fight without me disappearing on you."*

**Preparation**:
- Write out your opening sentence
- Practice saying it out loud alone
- Choose a calm moment (not during conflict)
- Commit to specific day/time
- Stay in the room for 10 minutes minimum, even if uncomfortable

**Success measure**: Did you speak your truth, even imperfectly?
