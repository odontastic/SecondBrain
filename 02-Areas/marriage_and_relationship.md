**Status:** Critical - Needs Serious First-Aid  
**Standard to Maintain:** Healthy, connected, supportive partnership

## Ongoing Responsibilities

### Daily Presence Practice
- **Evening presence (8:00-9:00 PM):** 60 minutes undivided attention
  - Devices in another room
  - Sit with wife during TV shows
  - Ask: "How are you feeling right now?" or "How was your day really?"
  - Listen without problem-solving
  - Eye contact, physical touch if receptive

- **Acknowledge her reality:** She carries enormous financial burden, feels alone, overwhelmed

### Communication Skills (First-Aid Kit)
- Use First-Aid Kit phrases during conflict:
  - "You're right. The pressure on you is immense."
  - "I can see how scary that is."
  - "I want to be on your team with this."
- Practice Sigh Playbook: Acknowledge her sighs
- Stay in room during conflict (no withdrawal)
- Speak even when uncomfortable

### Break Pursue-Withdraw Cycle
- Recognize withdrawal triggers
- Choose presence over protection
- Show her she's not alone
- Validate her experience without defensiveness

### Resentment Management
- Track resentment patterns
- Voice unspoken resentments
- Own my choices (I CHOSE house husband role)
- Weekly check-ins about resentment

## Standards

- Pursue-withdraw cycle improving
- Presence practiced daily
